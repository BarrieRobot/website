The next generation vending machine.

Barrie is the project name for the robotic bartender to be developed for Science Centre Delft. The Science Centre Delft acts as a gateway between the research projects of the Delft University of Technology and the public. It’s the place where innovations are proudly presented to the public and new technologies explained to children.

Currently, the coffee corner is traditional in its construction. To adhere to the technological and innovative spirit, the coffee corner will be given a remix! That’s where we come in.

Assignment

This project is part of the Minor Robotics from the TU Delft. The task given is to build a particular robot according to the requirements and whishes of the client. In our case, that is the Science Centre. The full minor description can be found here.

The Science Centre Delft is our employer. The Science Centre has the ambition to supply visitors an experience and education concerning new technologies developed at the TU Delft. Its vision is to have multiple robots in the building that assist and entertain the visitor. These robots should communicate with each other to give each user their own, personal experience.

Apart from our robot, another group is concurrently building a host robot. Its purpose is to provide information to visitors entering or leaving the building. See their project page, IONA. Other robots are meant to be added to the system later.

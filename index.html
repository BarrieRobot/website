Concept. What is that drawing?

The concept for this robot is an interactive, automated bar where you can order drinks and snacks and can sit or stand at. The table top is interactive, so users can choose their order by touching the table. This is achieved using a beamer and sensors. On the table there are three circles where the user can select if it wants a snack, cold drink or hot drink.

After choosing the category, the different options are one by one shown as holograms at the top of the bar. The user can swipe on the table to scroll through the different options. After selection and payment of a snack or drink, the hologram will “fall” down and an animation will be projected on the table top. This animation will draw the attention back to the table top and will make it look like the order comes rolling to the user. Also further information about the selected drink or snack could be projected. During the animation, the drink or snack is being prepared and moved to the front side of the table. As the animation also arrives at one of the three holes, the hole will open and the drink or snack will magically rise out of the table.

This bartender robot is an all-in-one solution for the Science Centre, using interesting technologies for the public and creating the ideal coffee experience!

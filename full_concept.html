Concept.

The concept for this robot is an interactive, all-in-one-piece, automated bar where you can order drinks and snacks and can sit or stand at. To make getting yourself a refreshment more entertaining, the following design was conceived.

Using a table top that is interactive, allows users to choose their order by touching the table surface. This is accomplished using a projector and vision technology. Three circles on the table are touch sensitive, selecting snacks, cold -, or hot drinks.

When choosing the category, various options are one by one shown on a holographic screen at the top of the bar. The table top projection can be used to scroll through the different products. After making a selection, the hologram will “fall” down on the hologram and seamlessly transition to the projected table top.

Drawing the attention to the table top, this gives the illusion that the order is transformed from a screen image to a real product.

During the animation, the drink or snack is prepared and moved to the front side of the table. As the animation also arrives at one of the three holes, the panel will open and the order will magically rise from underneath the table.

This bartender robot is an all-in-one solution for the Science Centre, using interesting technologies for the public and creating the ideal coffee experience!
